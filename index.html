Meet Your Facilitators

Danny Miranda

Danny Miranda was once making $10,000 a month and living in his dream location, San Diego. Despite having everything he thought he wanted, he found himself feeling unfulfilled and questioning his lack of enthusiasm for life.

At night, he would ask himself, “Why don’t I want to wake up in the morning? Why am I not excited about life? I have everything I thought I wanted. But it’s not enough.”

Driven by these unsettling feelings, he made a bold decision to quit his job. He began meditating and committing to the 75 Hard program daily. This journey of self-discovery led him to reach out to others, and one day, he posted on Twitter, “Who wants to talk on the phone?” The conversations he had with people around the world were so impactful that he decided to start recording them. In September 2020, he launched his podcast, a project that felt deeply aligned with his purpose. Within three months, he landed an interview with Gary Vaynerchuk, and within three years, his podcast had over 1,000,000 listeners.

Today, Danny helps guide others towards living with more freedom and embracing their true selves.

Nick Sweeney

Nick Sweeney is a high-performance breathwork coach specializing in helping professional athletes and business leaders unlock their potential. With expertise in the nervous system, breathwork, human health, and overcoming mental barriers, Nick’s journey began with a life-altering foot injury that abruptly ended his Division 1 ski racing career. When doctors told him he’d never walk properly again, Nick refused to accept that fate. Instead, he turned to unconventional healing methods, using breathwork, holistic health, and nervous system regulation to reclaim his mobility.

Today, Nick runs ultramarathons, hunts wild game, and, as he puts it, “breathes a lot.” His mission is clear: to manipulate, harness, and unleash the infinite power within the leaders of tomorrow.
